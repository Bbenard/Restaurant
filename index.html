About our Restaurant

Benah is South Africa’s favorite chain of seafood restaurants and now there’s one near you in Nairobi. At Benah you will find friendly people, a home-from-home feeling, and great value for your hardearned cash. One thing’s for sure. You will find only the freshest seafood at Ocean Basket. Plus, some nice wines to make a great meal even greater.Benah is for all da family, cos children love good seafood as much as you do. At Benah we love making waves of happiness with our delicious seafood.

Come on in and have a whale of a time!

“We wanted to make people aware of good seafood. We wanted to create a simple place where people could enjoy a great meal and great value. A homely place where they’d get together with friends and feel like they’re part of our family.”
